\# Dataset Integrity Notice



Please be advised that this folder does not contain the full 4,300+ images used in the study due to size constraints.



\### How to use this directory:

1\.  \*\*Check Samples:\*\* Sub-folders in `gold\_real\_d2/` contain sample files to show you how the SAM masks and LaMa restorations should be aligned with the raw images.

2\.  \*\*Restore Full Data:\*\* 

&#x20;   \*   To reproduce the full 150-epoch training, download the ISIC 2017/2018 sets and place them in `vmunt\_train\_base/`.

&#x20;   \*   To reproduce the benchmark scoring, download the full D2 set and place it in `gold\_real\_d2/`.

3\.  \*\*Naming Convention:\*\* Ensure all downloaded files follow the `ISIC\_XXXXXXX.png` format as shown in the provided samples.
